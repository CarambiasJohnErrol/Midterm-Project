About the Creator
I am John Errol Carambias, a student of University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines. Currently a second year student taking up Bachelor of Information Technology. Aside from my desire to become an I.T professional, I also want to be a succesful photographer someday.
I am from a small town called Tagoloan. Most of my photographs was taken here. Many people may know this place as a disaster as what they can see in the news. But i won't let those hearsay cover the undiscovered beauty of it. Its bad reputation won't erase its appeal. My favorite time of taking a picture in my town was during golden hour.
I also love experimenting with my pictures. I oftenly got sick if all of my photographs looks identical. So as a twist, i apply different effects and filter. Some examples of it are the glitch effect, double exposure and long exposure photography.
My favorite applications to post-process my photos are VSCOCam, Afterlight, Pollar and Snapseed. This mobile applications help me achieve the feelings that I want to convey in my photography. It may exaggerate or simplify the photo. These applications help me step-up my game even if I only use my phone in taking these pictures.
When I take photographs near my house, I have my companion with me. She is my niece, Angel. Since 2015, we love going to the dike every afternoon when we have free time. Because of that, I influenced her to like photography.
Photography for me was not just a form of art. It helps me de-stress myself from all of the difficulty that I am facing. It acts as an temporary escape from all of that troubles. It gives peace and calmness from all of the noise I hear near me.
